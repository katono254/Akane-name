Eichen Engine

Eichen name Engine,Developed by Greg Katono,is a web application that is able to do sorts of things.

The engine is able to take a users birthday and calculate the day he/she was born and later their eichen name will be outputed.

Eichen names are derived from Ghanian culture which in frequent occasions the name is derived from the day of the week.
